Philosophy of Karate Do

Karate Do is a martial art that originated in Okinawa, Japan, and is known for its emphasis on self-discipline, respect for others, and the pursuit of personal growth. The word Karate can be translated as 'empty hand', and Do means 'the way' or 'the path'. This reflects the philosophical underpinning of Karate Do, which is not only about learning physical techniques but also about developing the mind and spirit, and applying the principles of Karate in everyday life.
